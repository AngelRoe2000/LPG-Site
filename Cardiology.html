some reasons to visit a cardiologist

    Your Primary Care Physician has told you too!!
    - Even though we all hate the idea of any referal sometimes they are needed to ensure your health
    Family History
    -You sat down with your child to do a family tree and realised that Cardiac issues run in your family. Take the time to get checked out
    Your a Smoker
    -Here at LPG we will not be judging you for being a smoker. It does, however, come with some health issues that should be checked.
    Exercise
    -With the new year came some New Years resolutions. Before you begin that new exercise routine it is a good idea to have your heart checked first.
